Gaming

Gaming is who I am, it is a part of me. I grew up playing video games. Gaming has been accused of causing violence and brainwashing. In my case, gaming has been a way to escape to a different reality. I have had moments in my life where things were tough for me, and video games allowed me to release stress and in the process create great friendships.

Gaming against Depression and Anxiety

Gaming has been linked to helping fight depression and anxiety issues. I can only speak from my experience. In my case, when I was diagnosed with Diabetes at the age of 10, I was treated poorly from my peers at school. I was all of a sudden someone too fragile to play with. I became a loner over night. It was in these moments that gaming saved my life so to speak. It took me to another world where I can be anything I wanted and helped me cope with my own inner demons.

Content Creating

In my time of gaming I have grown a liking to content creating. What I mean, is creating youtube videos and also streaming on Twitch. I do it mainly because I like the process of making content and then editing and putting it out for the world to see. There have been a lot of influential streamers in my life, where they motivate you to do good in life not just for entertainment value.

I hope to be able to get a small following that I can inspire and motivate to tackle life and get a piece of their own success. We have a lot of negativity in this world, there needs to be a balance. I believe we are all capable of greatness and achievement. It takes a lot of hard work but the payoff is amazing.
