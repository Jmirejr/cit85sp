Vegeta: The Prince of all Saiyans

Born in the now extinct planet of Vegeta, his father was King Vegeta. His planet was destroyed by the space tyrant, Lord Frieza.

Vegeta is the known Prince of all Saiyans. He is at the moment the rightful heir to the throne.

Although he may be the heir to the throne, he is not the strongest saiyan in existence. That title falls to Goku.

Goku is the strongest there is but he wants no part in any throne, he just wants to fight strong foes.

In my personal opinion, I think Vegeta is the best character in the the Dragonball univerese. He had the most character development in the series. He went from villain to anti-hero, to me he shows the most human side out of all characters.
